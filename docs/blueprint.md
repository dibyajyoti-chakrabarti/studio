# **App Name**: MechHub

## Core Features:

- Design Upload: Allow users to upload STEP, STL, DXF, PDF, JPG, and PNG design files to cloud storage for manufacturing requests.
- Manufacturing Details Form: Collect manufacturing details such as process, material, quantity, surface finish, tolerance, delivery date, budget, and location.
- Vendor Matching: Match users with up to 6 suitable vendors based on process capability, location, and active status.
- Request for Quotation (RFQ): Allow users to select at least 3 matched vendors and submit an RFQ with their design and manufacturing details.
- Admin Quotation Generation: Enable administrators to review RFQs, enter final prices, lead times, and notes to generate quotations for users.
- Order Tracking Dashboard: Provide users with a dashboard to track their orders through various stages: RFQ Submitted, Under Review, Quotation Sent, In Discussion, Confirmed, In Production, Completed, and Closed.
- MechMaster Recommendation Tool: An AI tool helps the system recommend relevant MechMasters by interpreting the requirements provided by the user. The AI will determine when geographic location is of particular importance to the manufacturing requirements.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#2962FF), embodying trust and engineering precision.
- Background color: Dark steel grey (#232F34), providing a modern industrial aesthetic.
- Accent color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF), highlighting key interactions and call-to-actions with vibrancy.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif, used to convey a techy, mechanical feel for all headings. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif for clear and readable content, contrasting against the dark background. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Incorporate mechanical and industrial tech-themed icons for processes (CNC Machining, Laser Cutting, etc.).
- Implement a blueprint grid background overlay and Zomato-style vendor cards for a professional and innovative feel.
- Use subtle animated rotating gears in the hero section and smooth scroll animations throughout the application.
